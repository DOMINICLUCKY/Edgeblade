CHARACTER ABILITIES

Discover the unique powers and abilities of each elemental warrior

🔥 Kael Ignivar

FLAME ELEMENT

Ultimate: Inferno Surge

Unleash a raging inferno that devastates all enemies. Increases fire damage by 200% and grants temporary invulnerability for 3 seconds.

Weapon: Twin Ignition Blades

Dual blades engulfed in eternal flames. Each strike burns enemies and reduces their defense by 15%. Critical hit chance: 20%.

Active: Flame Dash

Swift movement leaving a trail of fire. Damage enemies along the path and gain a speed boost for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 8s.

🌪️ Aelith Stormborne

WIND ELEMENT

Ultimate: Tornado Vortex

Create a devastating tornado that pulls enemies together. All gathered enemies take 150% increased damage from next hit. Duration: 4s.

Weapon: Wind Sickles

Twin curved blades that cut through air and flesh. Critical strike chance increased to 25% with guaranteed follow-up hits. Attack speed: +30%.

Active: Wind Shield

Surround yourself with a protective wind barrier. Block 30% of incoming damage and reflect 5% back to attackers. Cooldown: 10s.

🌑 Morvyn Shadeveil

SHADOW ELEMENT

Ultimate: Shadow Mastery

Dissolve into pure shadow becoming invisible. Plan your next strike with strategic advantage and 300% bonus damage. Invisibility: 6s.

Weapon: Shadow Claymore

An oversized blade shrouded in darkness. Each hit steals 10% of enemy life and adds it to your health pool. Lifesteal: +10% damage.

Active: Void Step

Teleport through shadows to a target location. Leave behind a shadow clone to distract enemies for 3 seconds. Cooldown: 12s.
